About local.ch

local.ch is Switzerland's official phonebook directory. Available as printed phone book, online and on mobile devices. Our core competence is to bring people and businesses together.

With up to 4.3 million unique visitors and 19 million visits per month, local.ch is one of Switzerland's top websites.
