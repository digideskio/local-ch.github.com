Product Manager, User Experience

The User Experience Product Manager leads the implementation of www.local.ch, in terms of functional design, individual modules, screens and user features and ensures a permanent synchronization with Product Managers leading changes of our ad products portfolio and developing new monetization opportunities.

This role is required to perform complex business challenges. This will require a significant capacity for strategic thinking and quantitative analysis, hands-on involvement and an outstanding capacity to build positive relationships and trust over time through a strong commitment to high quality on time delivery.

Roles and Responsibilities

  • Understand the needs of local.ch users and web users in general
  • Translate broad strategic objectives into specific feature and product roadmaps and rapidly execute those plans
  • Develop and execute product roadmaps and implementation plans, conceive new user experience and features
  • Act as Product Owner to deliver changes on time
  • Establish and communicate priorities and make smart, informed tradeoffs
  • Completely immersed in both product management and user experience design
  • Work closely with interaction designer, visual designer and software engineers
  • Ensure planning and priorities synchronization with Ad Product Managers
  • Define, track and improve key product metrics and develop tests against those metrics

Desired Skills & Experience

  • Outstanding innovator and problem solver with top tier analytical abilities
  • Excellent strategic skills and ability to translate strategy into tactical plans
  • Proven project management and product management abilities
  • Capability to execute rapidly and efficiently in an unstructured environment
  • Outstanding team player with the ability to coordinate people and activities across multiple functional areas. Extensive track record in cross-functional collaboration
  • Ability to prioritize among competing opportunities, balance consumer needs with business priorities, and articulate the rationale behind decisions.
  • Demonstrated experience in conceiving user experience and user features for large audience websites
  • Experience in communicating through mock-ups, prototypes, flow diagrams, wireframe models and visual and written specifications
  • Aptitude for taking input from multiple sources and synthesizing it into coherent stories and designs
  • Ability to move easily between conceptual and detail activities
  • Proactive leader who takes initiative and demonstrates a high level of accountability
  • Effective and clear communication skills - both to internal teams and external stakeholders
  • Familiarity with web development technologies
  • BS/MS in Human Computer Interaction or related field preferred
  • German / English. French is a plus.

Place of work: Zurich

About local.ch

local.ch - The brand for directories from Swisscom Directories AG and LTV Gelbe Seiten AG

We bring people together - privately and commercially. Our core competence is in complete and - online - current directories: the telephone books (regional and local), on the internet at local.ch or on a mobile phone. The local.ch search platform offers plenty of local information but also a search for real estate.

The directories from local.ch - both printed and online - provide outstanding advertising opportunities for positioning a company and its offer in the right place. People who consult a directory generally have an actual need. By advertising in the directories from local.ch, you address consumers at a time when they are actually looking to buy.
