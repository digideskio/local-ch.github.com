Senior System Engineer

Our software engineers develop new features. On site. Agile. Using Ruby on Rails/Java/PHP. They like to deploy their new features and services often. System Engineering works closely together with Software Engineering to help build a fresh and reliable website

Our system serves 3.6 million unique clients a month, putting us among the top Swiss websites. We're also proud of our uptime. To achieve this we have a high level of redundancy (2 data centers, load balancing).

System engineering involves building and operating the hardware, software and network that runs our site.

In our team of 3 system engineers you would:

  • plan, develop and deploy hardware and systems according to technical requirements
  • simplify, consolidate and document complex systems
  • create cdist types and puppet recipes for the automated configuration of 160 servers.
  • solve technical problems, find and fix root issues
  • develop and improve our infrastructure so it enables us to rollout of new features quickly.
  • effectively ensure a high uptime of old and new systems
  • plan fallback scenarios in case of major outages (backups, rollbacks)

And you should have:

  • completed Fach-/Hochschulstudium
  • speak and write German and English fluently
  • experience writing script languages (Shell, Ruby or Python)
  • project experience
  • longtime experience in linux system engineering
  • experience in Ruby Java and PHP environments
  • knowledge of load-balancing and failover strategies
  • knowledge of code management tools (Git, Subversion)
  • fluent in TCP/IP, DNS and HTTP protocols
  • knowledge of monitoring, backups and replication of MongoDB, MySQL and PostgreSQL server
  • experience with virtualisation, ideally KVM
  • knowledge of web technologies (Javascript, Solr, Apache, Nginx)
  • experience in automation and measuring (Puppet, Nagios/Icinga, Graphite/Statsd, Splunk)
  • experience with cloud services (EC2, S3, Heroku, etc.)

In turn we offer you an environment where you can bring in your experience and know-how. As you see we enjoy using modern, agile technologies. Our flat hierarchy enables you to work you with a high degree of freedom and responsibility.

Place of work: Zurich, Switzerland
