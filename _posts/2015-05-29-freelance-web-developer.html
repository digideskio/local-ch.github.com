Freelance Web Developer

At local.ch we maintain and publish a lot of content online. We're looking for a freelance web developer who is experienced with HTML/CSS, and can help us to create new websites and maintain and update existing ones.

This position would be within the web team at local.ch, you would be working with experienced front- and back-end developers, designers and project managers to produce layout, content and design elements on web.

This is a part-time, freelance position, 1-2 days/week in Zurich, depending on workload. Together we would coordinate and agree on a schedule. We are hoping to find someone that can work on-site, to meet with developers and designers, although off-site work could be possible.

Since we are running one of the biggest web sites in Switzerland, you get a chance to have a positive impact on millions of users within your first day.

Desired Skills & Experience

  • Past experience building web sites and content online
  • HTML and CSS
  • Some Javascript familiarity
  • Understanding of responsive design and best practices
  • Image editing/preparation for web (Adobe Photoshop or similar)
  • Experience using web-based content management systems (CMS)
  • Experience turning design mockups into working web pages
  • Experience with a web development language, such as PHP a plus
  • Experience using Git a plus
  • Experience with Ruby/Rails, Liquid templates a big plus
  • Fluent in English. German/French/Italian is a plus.

Apply Today!

Please send an email with a link to your website or portfolio to jeremy.seitz@local.ch. Note: This is a freelance, part-time position that requires you to be legal to work in Switzerland.

Questions?

Questions about the job can be sent directly to the engineering team lead, Jeremy Seitz.
