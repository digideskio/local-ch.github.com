Mobile Web Engineer

As a mobile web engineer you will focus on enriching and creating a great user experience of local.ch mobile web applications. We base our development on newest Javascript mobile frameworks and combine a mix of agile practices, pair programming and code reviews to improve the process of working in a team. We take testing and automation seriously. As a small team, we believe we have constructed an environment that allows us to continue to adapt and develop cutting edge features.

Your responsibility is end-to-end, from planning, implementation, testing to maintaining your code after deployment. You are part of a solid team of engineers, working with product owners, specialists and other teams. New features are implemented and deployed in short iterations and your contributions and ideas are always welcomed.

While we are running one of the most popular mobile application in Switzerland, you get a chance to have a positive impact on millions of users within your first day.

Roles and Responsibilities

  • Delight users with great products.
  • Develop fast and responsive applications for mobile devices and tablets.
  • Assist product managers in understanding audience.
  • Transform mockups/designs into mobile-ready, optimized HTML/CSS/JavaScript.
  • Help improve the engineering tool chain and process.

  • Ideally 2+ years professional experience in web/mobile software development.
  • You love and hack JavaScript on a daily basis. Using Backbone.js or Angular.js is a plus.
  • You are comfortable delevoping apps for iOS, Android and/or another mobile platform.
  • You love and hack JavaScript on a daily basis. Using Backbone.js or Angular.js is a plus.
  • HTML5 and CSS3 are topics you read everyday about.

  • Excited by challenging problems, addicted to shipping features to production.
