Mobile Engineer / Junior Mobile Developer

Are you:

  1. An experienced mobile guru?
  2. Passionate about mobile development and eager to learn the ropes?

As a mobile web engineer you will focus on enriching and creating a great user experience on local.ch's "native" apps — with over 2 million downloads on iOS, Android and other platforms — and mobile web applications.

We base our mobile web development on the newest JavaScript mobile frameworks and combine a mix of agile practices, pair programming and code reviews to improve the process of working in a team. We take testing and automation seriously. As a small team, we believe we have constructed an environment that allows us to continue to adapt and develop cutting edge features.

On iOS, Android and other platforms such as Windows Phone, we have our reasons for going fully native.

Your responsibility is end-to-end, from planning, implementation, testing to maintaining your code after deployment. You are part of a solid team of engineers, working with product owners, specialists and other teams. New features are implemented and deployed in short iterations and your contributions and ideas are always welcome (and we love crazy ideas).

And because we're running some of the most popular mobile applications in Switzerland, you get a chance to have a positive impact on millions of users right from your first day.

Roles and responsibilities

  • Delight users with great products.
  • Develop fast and responsive applications for mobile devices and tablets.
  • Assist product managers in understanding audience.
  • Transform mockups/designs into mobile-ready, optimized HTML/CSS/JavaScript.
  • Help improve the engineering tool chain and process.

You wear at least some of these hats:

  • Bachelor's  degree  in  Computer  Science  (or  equivalent  experience) 
  • Ideally 2+ years professional experience in web/mobile software development.
  • You love and hack JavaScript on a daily basis. Using Backbone.js or Angular.js is a plus.
  • You are comfortable developing apps for iOS, Android and/or another mobile platform. Even better: you've actually published one or more apps.
  • HTML5 and CSS3 are topics you read everyday about.
  • Experience with any of the following scripting languages: PHP, Ruby or Python.
  • Experience with any of the following programming languages: Objective-C, Java, C, C++, C#.
  • You are not afraid of using Git.
  • Comfortable at a Unix command-prompt, ideally you are a Mac user.
  • Fluent in English (Italian, German, French, Spanish, Vietnamese, Romanian and/or Hindi is/are a definite plus). ☺
  • Can demonstrate skills in developing rich web/mobile applications and user interaction.
  • Excited by challenging problems, addicted to shipping features to production.
