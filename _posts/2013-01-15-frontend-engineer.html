Frontend Engineer

As a frontend engineer you have the chance to shape major parts of our frontend architecture and implementation. We base our development on the Ruby on Rails framework and combine a mix of agile practices, pair programming and code reviews to improve the process of working in a team. We are big fans of testing, employing Rspec, Capybara and Jasmine for testing. As a small team, we believe we have constructed an environment that allows us to continue to adapt and develop cutting edge features.

Your responsibility is end-to-end, from planning, implementation, testing to maintaining your code after deployment. You are part of an elite team of engineers, working with product owners, specialists and other teams. New features are implemented and deployed in short iterations and your contributions and ideas are always welcomed.

Roles and Responsibilities:

  • Completely immersed in agile product development and engineering efforts, working closely with product managers, interaction designers, visual designers, software engineers and architects
  • Develop web and responsive apps
  • Work with state-of-the-art search and storage systems
  • Assist product managers in writing user stories
  • Transform mockups/designs into cross browser HTML/CSS/JavaScript
  • Help improve site usability with A/B tests and data analysis
  • Improve our engineering tool chain
  • Help build great products

Desired Skills & Experience

  • Passion for the web and aware of new web trends
  • Ideally 3+ years work experience as a software engineer
  • Ruby/Rails experience
  • HTML and CSS (HAML/SCSS a plus)
  • Javascript skills, experience with MVC frameworks like Backbone, Ember.js or Meteor a plus
  • Understanding of HTTP, SSH, CGI, REST and all things web
  • Experience with at least one other programming language such as Java, Python, PHP or C++
  • Understanding of cross-browser development, testing and bug fixing
  • Passionate about testing
  • Comfortable at a Unix command-prompt.
  • Fluent in English. German/French is a plus.
