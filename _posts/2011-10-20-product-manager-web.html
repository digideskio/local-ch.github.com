Product Manager, Web

As a Product Manager Web you have a chance to create, develop, establish and maintain www.local.ch products that focuses on connecting consumers and local businesses (advertising, information and lead generation products). Your success is measured through the revenue and the profitability of the products.
As part of a highly motivated and open-minded team, you plan, develop and direct the efforts for the products that you own. You also take an active role in maintaining a consistent product portfolio and in assessing new opportunities.

This role is required to perform complex business challenges. This will require a significant capacity for strategic thinking and quantitative analysis, hands-on involvement and strong commitment to high quality on time delivery.

Roles and Responsibilities

  • Analyze customer needs and develop new product ideas or product improvement
  • Critically evaluate new products or product improvements ideas, test them and assess their market potential
  • Analyze and monitor competition and position product against competition
  • Develop business cases for new and existing products
  • Identify upsell opportunities
  • Produce product definitions, use cases and requirements
  • Define product pricing
  • Orchestrate product development and production process
  • Manage entire products life cycle from strategic planning to operational activities
  • Develop and implement company-wide go-to-market plans for new products and products changes introductions, working with all departments to execute
  • Ensure planning and priorities synchronization with other Product Managers
  • Define, track and improve key product metrics

Desired Skills & Experience

  • Outstanding problem solving and analytical skills
  • Excellent ability to translate strategy into tactical plans
  • Proven project management and product management abilities with end to end responsibility
  • Capability to execute rapidly and efficiently in an unstructured environment
  • Team player with the ability to coordinate people and activities across multiple functional areas. Extensive track record in cross-functional collaboration
  • Demonstrated experience in online advertising / online marketing product management, sales and/or marketing roles
  • Solid understanding of internet products, market and business models
  • Proactive person who takes initiative and demonstrates a high level of accountability
  • Effective and clear communication skills - both to internal teams and external stakeholders
  • BS/MS in marketing management and/or business administration
  • Language: German, reasonable level of English

Place of work: Zurich

About local.ch

local.ch - The brand for directories from Swisscom Directories AG and LTV Gelbe Seiten AG

We bring people together - privately and commercially. Our core competence is in complete and - online - current directories: the telephone books (regional and local), on the internet at local.ch or on a mobile phone. The local.ch search platform offers plenty of local information but also a search for real estate.

The directories from local.ch - both printed and online - provide outstanding advertising opportunities for positioning a company and its offer in the right place. People who consult a directory generally have an actual need. By advertising in the directories from local.ch, you address consumers at a time when they are actually looking to buy.
