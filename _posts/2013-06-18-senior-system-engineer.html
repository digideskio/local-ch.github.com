Senior System Engineer

Our software engineers develop new features. On site. Agile. Using Ruby on Rails/Java/PHP. They like to deploy their new features and services often. System Engineering works closely together with Software Engineering helping to build a fresh and reliable website.

Our system serves 4.3 million unique clients a month, making us the biggest national Swiss website. We're also proud of our uptime. To achieve this we have a high level of redundancy (2 data centers, load balancing) and a devops culture where Software and System Engineers help out keeping our services up.

System engineering involves building and operating the hardware, software and network that runs our site.

In our team of 3 system engineers you would:

  • plan, develop and deploy hardware and systems according to technical requirements
  • simplify, consolidate and document complex systems
  • create puppet recipes for the automated configuration of 160 servers (~100 hardware, rest VMs)
  • solve technical problems, find and fix root issues
  • develop and improve our infrastructure so it enables us to rollout new features quickly.
  • effectively ensure a high uptime of old and new systems
  • plan fallback scenarios in case of major outages (restore of backups, rollbacks)

You need to have completed either a Fach- or a Hochschulstudium and speak and write German and English fluently.
You should have these experiences:

  • linux system engineering (Debian, CentOS/RH)
  • network experience (routing, firewalls, vlan, dns)
  • virtualisation, ideally KVM
  • project planning, able to prioritise project work against operations work
  • setting up/inspecting ruby and java environments (e.g. unicorn, tomcat)
  • MongoDB: inspect, optimize, monitor, backup
  • load-balancing and failover strategies
  • writing of script languages (Shell, Ruby or Python)
  • code management tools (git, subversion)
  • automation and measuring (Puppet, Nagios/Icinga, Graphite/Statsd, Splunk)

We offer you an environment where you can bring in your experience and know-how. As you see we enjoy using modern, agile technologies. Our flat hierarchy enables you to work you with a high degree of freedom and responsibility.

Place of work: Zurich, Switzerland
