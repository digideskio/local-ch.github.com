Local Engineering Conference

The first-ever local.ch engineering conference will be at the Paul Scherrer Institut on September 2nd, 2013. It was created for local.ch engineers, technical project managers, and partners with the following goals:

  • Share information about services
  • Document our APIs
  • Develop standards and best practices
  • Improve cross-team collaboration
  • Promote engineering culture @ local.ch

The location is an active physics research facility. They will be providing us lunch, coffee, and a full tour with some activities later in the day.

Workshops

The workshops are done in parallel and repeat, with participants rotating between topics at their own pace. Each workshop topic is a hands-on exploration of a topic. Bring your laptop!
Topics:
  • API Design (debate our APIs, issues, and new ideas)
  • Team workflow (team tools and dev methods)
  • Round Table (open discussion about eng. topics)
  • Run the whole stack: Install the (Backend|Frontend|Mobile|Puppet) environment on your own system (with assistance from helpful team members).
