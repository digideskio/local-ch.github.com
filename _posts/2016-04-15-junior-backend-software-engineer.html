Junior Backend Java Developer

The backend team develops the infrastructure to import, process, and store the phonebook- and advertisement data for Switzerland’s official directory. We are providing a highly optimized and reliable layer of delivery services to make this data accessible to our search engine and the web/mobile platforms. Our main challenge is to constantly push our technology stack forward and to be ready for next-gen features.

As a member of the backend team, you will be part of a small but highly committed group of developers that is collaborating very closely with web- and mobile engineering teams to provide the best solutions possible to our users and customers.

Since we are running one of the biggest web sites in Switzerland, you get a chance to have a positive impact on millions of users within your first day.

Roles and Responsibilities

  • Translate business requirements into technical solutions
  • Develop and improve our application architecture
  • Take responsibility for all phases of the application life cycle
  • Do reviews and improve our code quality

Desired Skills & Experience

  • Development experience with Java (version 7+), even if in hobby projects (submit examples)
  • Spring framework knowledge is a plus
  • Versatile and always eager to tackle new problems and evolve our backend architecture
  • Highly interested in software development topics like industry trends, processes and development methods
  • Eager to learn and motivated to improve development skills
  • Basic understanding of API technologies, relational databases and document/key-value stores
  • You are a team player and have experience working in agile environments
  • Fluent in English; German/French/Italian is a plus

Apply Today!

Simply send your CV and a link to your GitHub account or code examples to hrz@localsearch.ch.
Note: This position requires that candidates are legal to work and live in Switzerland.

Questions?

Questions about the job can be sent directly to the web engineering team lead, Yannick Suter.
